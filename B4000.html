This is an editor I tried to make so I can edit parameters of my B4000+ unit in a graphic interface. I am not a coder and I basically copied oscillatorsink's code for the Volca Drum and slowly edited things for my unit.

Connect your B4000+ to your chosen MIDI interface or via USB.

Select that MIDI interface in the Midi Device Setup (default channel is 1).

The settings will be sent to your sound module automatically when the interface is chosen.

Make your patch!

My preferred way to "save" your patch is by clicking "Create Sharable Patch Link" and saving the link somewhere (and don't forget to share it!). You can also save a stream of MIDI CC data which can be played back using a DAW etc. by clicking "Save Patch". You can also save the current state of the patch on your B4000+.

Have fun!

If you have questions...I am probably not the one to ask, so I would point you to oscillatorsink. You can get hold of him on one of many social media platforms:

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
